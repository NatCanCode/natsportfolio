Hey, I'm Nathalie!

  • I'm a junior Web Developer living in Paris, previously working in international communications, and always up for learning new things.
  • At the crossroads between UX/UI design and Front-End Development, I aim to create neat, minimalist, and responsive websites. I strive for simplicity, clarity, and purity in my work.

  • I’m a coffee AND tea addict, inspired by my peers and colleagues, my little one, my partner, my grumpy cat, anything and everything photography related, and MedTech innovation.
  • I take pride in building and developing projects that can help make the world a better place.

    Selected projects

    Weather app

    Getting the weather in real time absolutely everywhere in the world! One of my first proper interactive web pages - coded in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and using two APIs.
    Want to try it?

    Simplon #Hackeuses Certification Award

    My very first website project - English and French pages - presented to a jury of professional web developers. Certification subject: "Women and tech - I code therefore I am".
    #Hackeuses jury prize awarded for the website design and subject originality.

    PhotoSwipe

    Teamwork on Design Thinking, persona, UX/UI design, and prototyping with Figma. The objective of this business unit project was to simulate, in scrum mode, the creation of a photo sorting platform, with speed and efficiency.

    Emoji Garden

    Emoji Garden game made with React.js translating UI requirements into React components. When you click the “Plant an Emoji” button, a new emoji appears in an available cell of the garden. An emoji will be planted as a sprout 🌱 It then automatically grows into a plant 🌿 then a treen 🌳 Clicking on an existing emoji will delete it from the garden.
    Would you like to play?

    Flour Shower

    Fully responsive HTML/CSS pages built from mockups. The job was not to re-design a 5-page bakery website but to faithfully implement the provided visuals with a clean code. This is where I got hooked on grids, and titles that overlap blocks!
    I got bonus points awarded for the embedded GoogleMaps information on the contact page!
    You can navigate through the project pages clicking on the buttons and the About, Contact, Unavoidable, Amazing links on the header and footer.

    Terra E-shop

    E-shop website project built at Simplon. Special focus made on UX/UI design from the landing page to the basket and payment pages.
    Mobile first approach, JavaScript title animation, and carousel.

    Image gallery animation

    Personal project on image gallery animation with JavaScript. Fluid image pop-up with a preview caption. The images are clickable - they expand when clicked and the caption is displayed.

    Letter animations

    Personal project on letter animations with JavaScript using JS libraries - Anime and ScrollReveal - and creation of a letter loader effect in case of poor network.
